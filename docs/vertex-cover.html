🛡️ Minimum Vertex Cover Finder Find minimum vertex covers with greedy, exact & König algorithms

A vertex cover of a graph G is a set S of vertices such that every edge has at least one endpoint in S. Finding a minimum vertex cover is NP-hard, but a simple greedy 2-approximation exists. For bipartite graphs, König's theorem gives an exact solution in polynomial time via maximum matching.
